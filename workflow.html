Video Editing Workflow Checklist

1. Client Onboarding

  • Discuss project scope, deadlines, and platform (YouTube, IG, TikTok, etc.)
  • Gather assets: video clips, brand materials, music preferences
  • Confirm pricing (based on selected package + add-ons)
  • Create project folder: /Clients/ClientName/ProjectName/

2. Send Invoice (Initial)

  • Invoice the client before starting
  • Optional: Require 50% upfront for larger jobs
  • Track invoice status (paid/unpaid)

3. Editing Phase

  • Import and organize footage
  • Perform rough cut
  • Add text overlays, effects, music, transitions
  • Add captions using Whisper + Whisper.cpp
  • Export first draft

4. Client Review & Revisions

  • Upload to Frame.io or preferred platform
  • Notify client and gather revision notes
  • Apply revisions (limit based on package)
  • If revisions exceed limit:
    • Inform client
    • Send invoice for extra revision ($10)

5. Final Approval

  • Export final version (H.264 for delivery, H.265 for backup)
  • Upload deliverables (final video, .srt file, thumbnail if included)
  • Deliver via Frame.io, Dropbox, Google Drive, etc.

6. Send Final Invoice (If not prepaid in full)

  • Include any add-ons or extra revision charges
  • Confirm client payment
